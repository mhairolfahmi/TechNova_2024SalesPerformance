# TechNova 2020-2024 SalesPerformance

## Company Background

Incorporated in 2005, TechNova has expanded its business portfolio across eight countries and now operates 57 physical stores along with one online platform. Today, TechNova offers extensive electronic products from computers, laptops and smart phones to household items like television and washing machines. In the commitment to prioritise customer convenience and innovation, TechNova has expanded its online business platform through TechGo e-commerce platform to integrate online and offline shopping experiences.

This report has been developed to analyse the sales performance for 2020 - 2024 covering 1 January 2020 to 31 December 2024, unless otherwise stated. This study provides comprehensive insight to the TechNova Internal team to improve overall sales team performance. The Key Insight in this report focus on following areas:

* Sales Trends: Focusing on key metrics of sales revenue, total orders, and average order value(sov) for 2020 - 2024.
* Regional Trends: Evaluating the sales performance across 8 different countries.
* Product Performance: analyze the F2024 sales data to identify which product category that generates the highest sales revenue.

## Insight Deep Dive

### Sales Trends

<img width="800" height="500" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cebb47bf-5423-4fa7-92a7-0ce575c1ab83" />


Sales Revenue
* TechNova recorded its highest monthly sales in December 2023, reaching $2.45M, which is nearly 2.7 times above the average monthly sales of $0.92M.
* Monthly sales revenue in both 2022 and 2023 consistently outperformed the average monthly sales benchmark of $0.92M, indicating the effectiveness of the company’s marketing campaigns and a shift in consumer behaviour during the post-COVID period.
* Sales began to decline in early 2024, with a particularly sharp drop from February 2024($2.12M) to March 2024($0.73m), which may be attributed to economic slowdown and market saturation.

Total Orders
* The high fluctuations in TechNova’s sales revenue were primarily driven by changes in total orders, indicating that both the growth in 2023 and the decline in 2024 were largely due to variations in order volume.
* In December 2023, TechNova recorded 1,237 total orders before the numbers began to decline in 2024, reaching only 288 total orders by December 2024.

Aov
* Aov is remained relatively stable  at average 2.17K from 2020 to 2023, peak at april 2024 at 3.23K and lowest at 1.54K at october 2024.
* Despite strong growth in total orders and sales revenue during 2022 and 2023, AOV consistently declined year-over-year at range -1% to -7%.
* This indicatingmost of the sales transaction is tended to be in smaller size or lower in spends.
