# TechNova_2024SalesPerformance

## Company Background

Incorporated in 2005, TechNova has expanded its business portfolio across eight countries and now operates 57 physical stores along with one online platform. Today, TechNova offers extensive electronic products from computers, laptops and smart phones to household items like television and washing machines. In the commitment to prioritise customer convenience and innovation, TechNova has expanded its online business platform through TechGo e-commerce platform to integrate online and offline shopping experiences.

This report has been developed to analyse the sales performance for F2024 covering 1 January to 31 December 2024, unless otherwise stated. This study provides comprehensive insight to the TechNova Internal team to improve overall sales team performance. The Key Insight in this report focus on following areas:

* Sales Trends: Focusing on key metrics of sales revenue, total orders, and average order value(sov) for F2024.
* Regional Trends: Evaluating the sales performance across 8 different countries.
* Product Performance: analyze the F2024 sales data to identify which product category that generates the highest sales revenue.

* After achieving its highest sales revenue in 2023, TechNova saw its revenue decline in 2024 by about 50% compared to its best year at only $9.26M.
* This decline was driven by a drop in total orders, which fell from 9,129 in 2023 to 4,590 in 2024, representing a 50% decrease. 
* Even though both sales revenue and total orders declined, the average order value (AOV) remained stable at $2.02K, showing only a 1% difference from 2023.
